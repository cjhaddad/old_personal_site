Blocs, Procs, and Lambdas!

March 15, 2015

Lambdas require the exact number of arguments within it to be passed to it, when it is called. Whereas Procs do not. Procs can be passed zero arguments, even when it has arguments within it. If you try to call a Lambda without a needed argument, you will get an error. If you call a Proc without an argument, no error will occur.
